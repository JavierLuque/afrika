Our Botswana Classic Mobile Camping Safari takes you through best route through northern Botswana covering all the main wildlife areas.

This is a routed safari that starts in Maun (the safari capital of Botswana) and ends in Kasane. This is the reverse of our other safari which covers the same route and takes you through some of the most famous regions in Africa. The safari starts in Maun and takes you through the famous Moremi Game reserve with its dense predator numbers and permanent Okavango Delta water channels. Continuing through to the Khwai Community Concession which has been a major wildlife attraction for a numbers of years, the safari heads on to Savute where the channel is now flowing once more and finally ending up at the Chobe River Front. This safari takes in all the classic destinations that Northern Botswana has to offer.
